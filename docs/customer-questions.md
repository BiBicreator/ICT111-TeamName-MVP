# Customer Discovery Questions

## Project Title

RSU Campus Buddy (Smart Campus Chatbot for Student Services with Image Recognition)

## Target Respondents

* Freshmen (1st-year students)
* Current RSU students
* Campus visitors

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT111 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary, and your responses will be kept anonymous. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Have you ever had difficulty finding a building, classroom, or campus information at RSU?

4. Please describe the most recent situation when this happened.

5. What information were you trying to find?

* Building location
* Facilities and services
* Operating hours
* Student clubs and activities
* Campus regulations
* Other: _______

## Current Workaround

6. How do you currently solve this problem?

* Ask friends or senior students
* Ask university staff
* Search the RSU website
* Search social media
* Use Google Maps
* Other: _______

7. Are these methods effective for you? How satisfied are you with these methods?

8. What is difficult, slow, confusing, or frustrating about the current methods?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

9. How often do you experience this problem?

* Daily
* Weekly
* Monthly
* Rarely
* Never

10. On a scale of 1–5, how serious is this problem for you? Why?

11. What happens because of this problem?

* Waste of time
* Stress or frustration
* Arrived late to class or activities
* Missed important information
* Had to ask multiple people for help
* No significant consequence
* Other: _______

12.How important is it to have a faster way to access campus information?

## Closing

12. If there were a system where you could upload a photo of a campus building and instantly receive information about it, would you use it?
 
13. Which features would be most useful? (Select all that apply)
* Building identification from photos
* Building information and facilities
* Operating hours
* Student club information
* Chatbot for questions
* Campus map and directions
* Frequently asked questions

14. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?
